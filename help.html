Before Reporting

Useful details

  • Add a screenshot if something looks broken.
  • Mention whether you were on the live site or the local app.
  • Include your browser if the issue happened on the website.
  • Say what tab or mode you were using when it happened.

Quick FAQ

Common questions

  • Browser progress is stored locally on that device unless you export it.
  • You can move progress between devices by exporting and importing your save file.
  • Some item images come from Stardew Valley Wiki URLs, so they may need internet access to appear.
  • Hoard mode is separate from the regular perfection tracker and does not count toward the main counters.
