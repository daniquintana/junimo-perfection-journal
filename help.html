Guide, help & feedback

Help Page

This page explains what the tracker covers, how to use each tab, and the few important limits to keep in mind. This tracker is still a work in progress, so feel free to report bugs or send suggestions.

What This Is

  • A browser-based checklist for reaching True Perfection in Stardew Valley 1.6.
  • Built to track your progress, not to replace the wiki or a full game guide.
  • Best for one farm at a time, with export/import if you want to swap between farms or devices.
  • Includes Dangerous Hoarding Pit as a separate stash helper.

How To Use

Basics

  • Use the main tabs to track each part of perfection.
  • Use search and filters to narrow what is on screen without changing the real totals.
  • Use export/import to move your tracker data between browsers, devices, or farms.
  • Use the hoard button if you want a separate stash tracker for shared item needs.

General

  • Use the General tab as the main dashboard for your farm.
  • The Perfection card shows your overall completion, and the smaller cards break down the official perfection categories.
  • Use it to see what still needs attention before jumping into a specific tab.

Fish

  • Track every fish needed for perfection.
  • Filter by place, season, weather, and status to narrow the list.
  • Check a fish off once it has been caught.

Cooking

  • Track cooked recipes and the ingredients needed to finish them.
  • Switch between Recipes, Planner, and Split depending on whether you want the checklist, the ingredient totals, or both.
  • Use the planner inputs to record ingredients you already have without marking a recipe cooked too early.

Crafting

  • Check off every crafting recipe needed for perfection.
  • Search or filter by status when you want to focus on unfinished crafts.
  • The list follows the in-game collection order.

Shipping

  • Track every item that must be shipped at least once.
  • Use search and status filters to show all items, only shipped items, or only what is still missing.
  • Check an item once it has been shipped.

Museum

  • Track all 95 artifact and mineral donations in museum order.
  • Check each donation off once it has been turned in.
  • Reward progress comes from the museum items you have checked.

Other Perfection

  • Use this tab for friendships, monster goals, skills, stardrops, walnuts, obelisks, and the Gold Clock.
  • Enter counts where the tracker asks for current hearts, levels, kills, or walnuts.
  • Use checkboxes for goals that are simply done or not done.

Dangerous Hoarding Pit

  • Use it as a separate stash tracker for items needed across cooking, crafting, shipping, and obelisks.
  • Search or filter by category when you want to focus on one kind of stockpile.
  • Its counters are separate from your regular perfection tracker.

What It Tracks

  • The main tracker covers fish, cooking, crafting, shipping, museum donations, friendships, monster goals, skills, stardrops, walnuts, obelisks, and the Gold Clock.
  • It also includes export/import for tracker progress and a separate hoard mode for shared item totals.
  • Counts are based on the exact items or actions needed for the tracker itself, not on extra copies you may want for profit, storage, decorating, or convenience.
  • It does not track side costs or optional upgrades like bridge repairs, raccoon requests, house upgrades, or extra crafted objects beyond what the tracker asks for.

Pay Attention To

  • Browser progress is stored locally on that device unless you export it.
  • Some item images load from Stardew Valley Wiki URLs, so an internet connection helps them appear.
  • Hoard mode is separate from the regular perfection tracker and counts exact ingredients only, so processed items like bars are not automatically rolled up from raw materials like ore.
  • Phone and tablet support is still a little temperamental in a few spots, so please double-check your entries and do not be shy about telling me if something looks off.

Feedback / Issue Submission

  • Use the feedback form if the numbers seem clearly wrong, something does not save, or a page looks broken.
  • Add a screenshot if something looks broken.
  • Mention whether you were using the tracker on a computer/laptop or a phone/tablet.
  • Include your browser if the issue happened on the website.
  • Say what tab or mode you were using when it happened.
